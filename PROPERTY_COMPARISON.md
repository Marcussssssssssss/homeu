# 🔀 Property Comparison Feature

The **Property Comparison** feature allows tenants to compare up to 2 rental properties side-by-side to make informed decisions.

## 📊 How It Works

1.  **Select Properties**: On the property listing page, click the compare icon (☑️) on the top-left of any property card.
2.  **Compare Button**: A floating button with a badge appears at the bottom right once you select at least one property.
3.  **View Comparison**: Click the button to open the comparison screen.
4.  **View Modes**: Visual side-by-side cards with detailed comparison rows.

## ✨ Key Features

*   **2-Property Limit**: Focused comparison between your top two choices.
*   **Price Range Highlight**: Shows min/max prices and calculated potential savings.
*   **Image Gallery**: Each card includes the property's image carousel.
*   **Side-by-Side Details**: Compares Location, Type, Rooms, Furnishing, Owner, and Landmarks.
*   **Management**: easily remove individual properties or clear all selections.
